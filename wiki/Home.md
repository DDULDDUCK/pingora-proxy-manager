- **High Performance**: Built on Cloudflare's Pingora framework.
- **Zero-Downtime**: Update configurations without restarting the server.
- **Automated SSL**: HTTP-01 and DNS-01 (Wildcard) support via Let's Encrypt.
- **L4 Streams**: TCP and UDP port forwarding.
- **Access Control**: IP whitelisting/blacklisting and Basic Auth.
